### Main Idea
- This paper focuses on explicitly describing the group scheme `Lau^{d,d'}_n`, which bands the gerbe structure of the morphism `\phi_n : BT^{d,d'}_n -> Disp^{d,d'}_n`. The morphism `\phi_n` is a canonical morphism from the stack of n-truncated Barsotti-Tate groups (`BT^{d,d'}_n`) to the stack of n-truncated displays (`Disp^{d,d'}_n`) over Fp. The paper expands on Lau's previous work by providing an explicit description of this group scheme, which was previously only known to exist and have certain properties.
- The authors also generalize Lau's result by relating the stacks of n-truncated Barsotti-Tate groups to that of n-truncated displays for the case related to any pair (G,µ) where G is a smooth group scheme over Z/p^nZ and µ is a 1-bounded cocharacter of G. In particular, the authors provide conjectural descriptions of the stack of n-truncated Barsotti-Tate groups over Spf Zp, and for its generalization.
