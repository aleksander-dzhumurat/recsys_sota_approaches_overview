## 🔗 Access Options for ACM-Only Papers

1. **ACM Digital Library** — institutional or personal membership: [dl.acm.org/conference/recsys](https://dl.acm.org/conference/recsys)
2. **Author pages** — many authors post final PDFs on personal/lab websites
3. **Google Scholar** — search the title; often links to author-hosted PDFs
4. **Semantic Scholar** — [semanticscholar.org](https://www.semanticscholar.org) aggregates open-access versions
5. **Email authors directly** — authors are generally happy to share preprints
